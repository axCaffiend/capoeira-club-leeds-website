What to expect

Music

We start the first 30 minutes or so learning a capoeira song together and practising how to use different instruments - such as the atabaque (drum), berimbau, and pandeiro (tambourine). No musical experience is required, just enjoy!

Movement

The main part of our session - after a quick warm up, we focus on learning or refining moves, then combine them to create a sequence. Expect a full workout and feel free to ask for help with anything you're unsure about.

Roda

We finish with a roda, where we play short capoeira games within a circle. This is a chance to practise what we've learnt in class or try new techniques - it's not formal so we encourage everyone to play and try out what we've covered in the class within a game.
